Send Voucher

Send vouchers to your recipients by uploading their details in a CSV file.
Follow the steps below to complete your voucher distribution.

How To Send Voucher

1

Step 1: Set Up Your Voucher Send

Enter the total number of recipients.
Select up to three (3) voucher types, and specify how many vouchers each recipient will receive.

2

Step 2: Download the Recipient Template

Download the CSV template and fill in your recipients' details.
These details are required to deliver vouchers to recipients’ TGV App Wallet.

3

Step 3: Upload and Send

Upload the completed CSV file to proceed.
Once uploaded, vouchers will be sent based on the information provided.
